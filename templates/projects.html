Guitar Effects Pedals

I have worked on and off for years building, designing, and modifying effects pedals. Originally inspired by circuit bending cheap pedals and finding interesting results, I moved on to trying a pedal kit. It worked well, and I have made and modified several pedals since. Some pedals include a ZVez Super Hard On and RAT clone combined into one pedal, a circuit bent Arion chorus that can generate "whale like" tones, and many clones (MXR Phase 90, MXR Blue Box, TS-808).
