Originally started with tinkering with trying to make effects in Arduino-C, the idea for guitar++ has been growing and is in beginning development stages. After finding it rather difficult to create effects and tones with such little computing power, I decided to take a different route. I was invited and accepted a position as a teaching assistant in Electronic Musical Instrument Design, and decided to use the same pipeline of Arduino Max Reason. This page will be updated as this is a work in progress alongside my students for the Spring 2015 Semester.
